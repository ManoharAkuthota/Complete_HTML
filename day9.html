iframe: this tag is used to play the  videos in our webpage, we can also other web applications on our webpage by using this tag.

            Embedding videos: applications like youtube provides a way to share there videos on our webpage with the help of embedded link. TO get that link we have click on the share button and next click on the embed, that will provide a link and paste that link wherever you want on the webpage.

            Attributes like: 
            Controls: It will provide the controls like pause & resue for the video. 
            Autoplay: the video will play automatically in a loop.
